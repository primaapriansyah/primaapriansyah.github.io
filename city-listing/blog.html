From Beach Glitz to Urban Ritz, Miami's Hotels Shine
Feb 12, 2017
Miami's hotel scene can be described by the same word that describes its climate: hotter than hot. All over the Magic City, you'll find accommodations ranging from the ever-so-shady hourly motel to the world-class, five-star resorts that attract celebrities and business moguls from all over. From Sunny Isles to Coconut Grove, there are hotels for every taste and budget in Miami. While some places may simply offer perks like airport shuttles and fitness centers, you'll quickly discover that in many Miami hotels, it's just as easy to find everything from poolside cabanas and private hot tubs to in-room spa treatments and fine dining experiences.

Flashlight Tours Arrive at Phoenix's Desert Botanical Garden
Jan 17, 2017
This summer, visitors to Phoenix can enjoy exploring the Desert Botanical Garden at night with the return of the garden's popular flashlight tours. These self-paced night tours are designed to let you see, hear and feel the desert at night. Ten discovery stations along the garden's main trail are set up to evoke the diversity of desert night life. Kids and families will enjoy learning about the secret nocturnal habits of saguaros, night bloomers, tarantulas, scorpions, lizards, snakes, night birds, bats and other desert plants and creatures.
